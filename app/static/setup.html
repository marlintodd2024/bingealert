How to find your X-Plex-Token:
Sign in at app.plex.tv, open any media item, click ⋯ → Get Info → View XML. The browser URL that opens contains ?X-Plex-Token=<your-token> — copy the value after the equals sign. Full guide: support.plex.tv.
